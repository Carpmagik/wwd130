Origins and Location - Jotchua Con is an event that celebrates Jotchua. There is merchandise you can buy, like bags or phone cases to remember your time. It started back in 2016, and while niche, people still come together and have a good time. The original founders were a group of Mexican friends that came together and taped up Jotchua Con Posters around. It only gained popularity from there. Dancing and singing is a blast with a dedicated DJ, there is even a quiet place if you want to lay low and play some board games! The event is being held at 1234 Spark Street, Electri City, LA 90210 . It's in the Thunder Volt Recreation Center.
Who Can Enter - Jotchua Con is free, anyone can enter. There is a potluck where you can bring food and enjoy other flavors of food as well at no cost. This event is fun for the whole family. Bring your kids, pets, and friends! There are even various games and activities to partake in. It is a great way to meet new people who share the same love for Jotchua as you do! Forge lasting relationships with other Jotchua fans and make memories that will last a lifetime!
What to Bring - Jotchua Con always need more plates and napkins, however do not be that person that only brings plates and napkins or we all would be eating paper for dinner. Desserts of all kinds are welcome, including pies and cake. Some people have brough tacos, anything edible is welcome. Go to the store and buy a bag of chips, anything works! Bust out your best recipe! Make sure to label any food that may have allergens in it, so everyone can have a safe and fun time.
Fun Activities - There are various games and activities to partake in. There is a photo booth where you can take pictures with life-sized cutouts of Jotchua and his friend. There is also a Jotchua look-alike contest where the winner gets a lifetime supply of Jotchua merchandise. There is even a Jotchua trivia contest, so brush up on your Jotchua knowledge before attending!
Merchandise - There is a variety of merchandise you can buy at Jotchua Con. From stickers to phone cases, there is something for everyone. There are even limited edition items that are only available at the con. So make sure to bring some extra cash if you want to take home a piece of Jotchua Con memorabilia! Great for Christmas gifts, birthdays, or just because you love Jotchua!
