Who is Jotchua - Jotchua, otherwise known as Perro Dinero, has massed a cult following in the online world. In March of 2021, two puppies were put up for adoption in a now privated Facebook account. Many posts online edit the main picture of the yellow-furred dog into a funny meme. Jotchua became more mainstream when Spanish internet users started using the dog, known then as "Perro Dinero" (Money Dog), as their profile pictures. It was said that those who had the dog in their profile they would be awarded money. It is unknown whether people actually believed this or wanted to take part in the joke.


Who is his Friend - The other dog, Jotchua's friend, is less known but just as cute. The two dogs are inseparable and are often seen together in videos and pictures that have been photoshopped. The original post of Jotchua's friend shows the dog hugging a piece of bamboo. Editors online have altered the image so instead the two dogs are hugging each other. People online have named him Jaekook, a name originating from Japan. Internet users have adored this duo ever since their post went viral.
Jotchua Con - Jotchua Con is free, anyone can enter. There is a potluck where you can bring food and enjoy other flavors of food as well at no cost. There is even a soup tasting contest that is extrememly spicy! There are mild options, of course, if spice isn't your thing. There will be a Jotchua look-alike contest where the winner gets a lifetime supply of Jotchua merchandise. There are also various games and activities to partake in. There is a photo booth where you can take pictures with life-sized cutouts of Jotchua and his friend. More info on next page.
