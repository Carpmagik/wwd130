Other Popular Dogs

PIBBLE - Pibble has recently gained popularity, and often overshines Jotchua. Whenever they are shown side by side, most people assume Jotchua is a version of Pibble, similarly to the alien species of Geeble. It should be cleared right away that Jotchua, is not, Pibble. Pibble came to fame through the iconic phrase, "Wash my bellah". This has amassed fans from around the world, however I am here to say that Jotchua is way better due to the latin culture Jotchua brings to the table.
GEEBLE - Geeble is a version of Pibble from another planet that is green. However, this is a clever ploy that tricks the consumer mind. It is like how Sun Chips has different versions of flavors, making seem like they have a varity. However, underneath is just the same, unoriginal flavor even if the bag says "Garden Salad". It tastes the same as any other Sun Chip, just with a different coat of paint. Jotchua is not like that. He is the same no matter what, often paired with his friend that inspires hope in people.
WHY YOU SHOULD CARE- Other competitors, like Pibble, Geeble, or Jaekook, have taken the spot like away from Jotchua. Their online presence is massive compared to Jotchua, even if Jotchua has been out for longer. There are reasons Jotchua is still vastly surperior to his competition. Jotchua, who has even been seen along side Geeble, maintains his relevance. The people's love for Jotchua illustrates that even after years of the internet cycle pushing out new trends, Jotchua remains at the heart of many.

Doge became popular in 2013, his image often being accompanied by comic sans text. Doge is a Shiba Inu from Japan, his real name being Kabosu. The meme became popular due to the dog's amusing facial expressions. Kabosu died in May of 2024 at age 18.

Cheems was a dog back in 2017. It was adopted into a family in Hong Kong, his image gaining popularity during Covid. The term "Cheemsburger" was popularized during this time. Unfortunately, Cheems died at age 12 after heart surgery.

Loki was popularized in 2023 due to his image of his eyes closed circulating around the internet. People have used this image to express the emotion of second hand embarrassment to something cringe. Loki died in 2024.
