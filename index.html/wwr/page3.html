This has amassed fans from around the world, however I am here to say that Jotchua is way better due to the latin culture Jotchua brings to the table.

It tastes the same as any other Sun Chip, just with a different coat of paint. Jotchua is not like that. He is the same no matter what, often paired with his friend that inspires hope in people.
WHY YOU SHOULD CARE- Other competitors, like Pibble, Geeble, or Jaekook, have taken the spot like away from Jotchua. Jotchua, who has even been seen along side Geeble, maintains his relevance. The people's love for Jotchua illustrates that even after years of the internet cycle pushing out new trends, Jotchua remains at the heart of many.
