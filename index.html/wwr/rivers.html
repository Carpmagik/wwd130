Explore Our Rivers

The Salmon River - This river is calm and gentle, great for beginners and families. It offers beautiful scenery and a relaxing experience. The gentle breeze and the sound of flowing water create a peaceful atmosphere for all ages. Its tranquil path winds through lush forests, past quiet villages, and alongside wildlife habitats spotting deer and birds is common. Fishing is allowed with permits; many enjoy picnicking on its banks or simply floating down in inner tubes on warm summer days. Kayaking and canoeing are popular rentals available nearby for all skill levels. T he river's source is stunning a crystal-clear spring bubbling from the earth; its end meets a serene lake perfect for sunset views. Locals claim the river has healing properties thousands visit annually for supposed spiritual renewal.
Willows's Nest - Seated beneath the Gigoa Mountains, Willow's Nest offers a thrilling experience with its moderate rapids and stunning views. Perfect for those seeking adventure while still being relatively safe. Its winding course cuts through rocky canyons and alongside towering trees scenery is breathtaking. Kayaks and rafts are ideal. Waterfalls and swimming holes dot the riverbank popular spots to relax and picnic. Local guides offer tours sharing stories of Native American artifacts found nearby and hidden caves behind waterfalls. Whether this is true or not is unkown, what is clear, however, is how fun the river is to explore!
Death's Spiral - This hardcore river is suitable for Adventurers seeking an intense and challenging rafting experience. With its powerful rapids and unpredictable currents, it is not for the faint of heart. The beginning part is calm; flat and easy to navigate. However, as you progress, the river transforms into a wild ride filled with thrilling drops and sharp turns. The river cuts through deep gorges and past towering cliffs, harsh waves crashing against rocks. Only experienced rafters should attempt this river; safety gear is a must. Local guides provide essential safety briefings and equipment rentals. If you are brave enough to attempt and conquer Death's Spiral, you will be rewarded with a free T-shirt!. Nothing says a vacation well spent as getting stuff for free!
